About

Photova is an interactive, dynamic, photography portfolio. It gives professional photographers a platform to display and share their work in an elegant and simple way.

How it works

The photographer and admin can upload photos, manage galleries by different collections keeping the portfolio organized according to their liking. These can then be assigned to specific users in order to share private galleries with clients.
