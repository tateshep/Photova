About

Photova is an interactive, dynamic, photography portfolio. It gives professional photographers a platform to display and share their work in an elegantly simple way.

How it works

The photographer and admin can upload photos and organize the gallery by different collections. These can then be assigned to specific users in order to share private galleries accessible by the end user under "My Collections".
